# The $568,059 Question

How Sacramento closed a $66.2 million deficit by firing 26 park workers, eliminating the mounted police unit, and closing four wading pools, while expanding the Office of Diversity and Equity by 9.9 percent and standing up a brand-new diversity recruiting unit inside the Fire Department.

**Read the full investigation:** [https://dont-shoot-the-data.github.io/sacramento-budget/](https://dont-shoot-the-data.github.io/sacramento-budget/)

## What the budget shows

In the FY2026/27 Proposed Operating Budget released April 29, 2026, Sacramento City Manager Maraskeshia Smith fired 26 Park Maintenance Workers to save $567,428. In the same document, she gave the Office of Diversity and Equity a 9.9 percent raise to $568,059. The two figures are $631 apart.

The cuts that hurt frontline workers and services total $13 million. The equity-coded bureaucracy and slush funds the City Manager left in place total $48 million. Eliminating that spending would close most of the deficit without firing a single park worker. She made the opposite choice.
